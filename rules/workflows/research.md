# Research Policy

Research **Project Artifacts** capture analysis, trade-offs, and investigations.

## When to use

* Ambiguous requirements.
* Multiple technical options.
* Performance or Security investigations.

## Workflows

* **Create**: Use `workflows/research.md` when you need to investigate something.

## Lifecycle

1. **Active**: Investigation in progress.
2. **Concluded**: Analysis complete. (Link to resulting ADR/Plan).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Traceability**: Always link the Research back to the document that triggered it.
